Our Story

The Asphalt Calculator is built for the modern human’s, the go-getters, DIY enthusiasts and smarter professionals!

Launched in 2024, our Asphalt Calculator is built on the vision to make construction calculations accessible and accurate for everyone.

Let’s face it, math is hard, especially in the real world, where money is involved. So, we understand the importance of accurate calculations. So, to simplify the construction business, we developed this user-friendly and precise calculator to help you achieve the best near to accurate estimates.

The asphalt calculator was built by Prince Singh and his team of experts in 2024. Prince is an esteemed developer with years of experience in the software and IT industry. He embodies a relentless pursuit of innovation, which he applies to his work and reverse engineers everything around him in the quest for discovery.

His characteristic includes a unique blend of technology and passion for empowering with tools.
